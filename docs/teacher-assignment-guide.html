üìã Quick Start Guide

1

Select a Teacher

Use the dropdown menu at the top of the page to select the teacher you want to manage. The page will refresh and show their current assignments and statistics.

2

View Statistics

See a quick overview with 4 stat cards showing: Total Subjects, Total Classes, Total Assignments, and Class Teacher Status. This gives you an instant snapshot of the teacher's workload.

3

Batch Assignment (Recommended for initial setup)

Click "Manage Assignments" to open a modal where you can select multiple subjects and classes at once. Check the boxes for subjects the teacher can teach and classes they'll be teaching. Click "Save Assignments" when done.

4

Specific Assignment (For detailed tracking)

Click "New Assignment" to assign a teacher to teach a specific subject in a specific class. This creates a detailed record tied to the current academic session and term.

5

Manage Assignments

View all assignments in the table. Remove any assignment by clicking the trash icon. Use the subjects and classes sections to see overviews of what the teacher is assigned to.
